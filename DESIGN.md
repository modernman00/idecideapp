# iDecide Design System (Budget Boss Inspired)

## Overview
A clean, high-whitespace design system inspired by the Google Stitch "Budget Boss" preview. Focused on clarity, professional financial guidance, and a premium "vibe".

## Principles
1. **High Whitespace**: Use generous padding and margins to create a sense of calm and clarity.
2. **Professional Green**: Use dark forest green as the primary color to evoke trust and financial stability.
3. **Ambient Atmosphere**: Use soft, pulsing background glows at the screen edges to provide an AI-native feel.

## Usage
- **Cards**: Use white cards with `rounded-lg` and subtle shadows.
- **Buttons**: Use pill-shaped buttons with dark green backgrounds and trailing arrow icons.
- **Progress**: Use a slim horizontal progress bar at the top of the evaluation flow.
